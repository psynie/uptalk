How do I get started with coaching?
+
Book any of my 1:1 sessions to start. We'll discuss your goals, current position, and create a personalized roadmap. Most clients see results within 30 days.
What's the difference between your services?
+
1:1 meetings are for personalized coaching, Webinars for group learning, Cohorts for 30-day immersive programs, and Digital Products are self-paced resources. Choose based on your learning style and timeline.
Can I switch roles from non-sales to sales?
+
Absolutely! I've successfully coached 200+ professionals from non-sales backgrounds into tech sales. Your background is an asset. We'll highlight transferable skills and position you for entry-level AE roles.
What's your success rate?
+
100% of my clients who complete the 30-day sprint have landed roles within 60 days. Average placement time is 45 days, and average salary package is $80K-$120K base + commission.
How do you handle different time zones?
+
I'm available for sessions across major time zones - IST, PST, EST, and UTC. We can schedule sessions at your convenience. Recordings are provided for any session you can't attend live.
What if I need help after the coaching ends?
+
All my clients get lifetime access to our community channel and resources. You can reach out anytime for quick questions, and I offer follow-up sessions at discounted rates.
